Key Findings
Answering the Research Questions
💀
As dehumanization goes up, does violence go up?
Yes — strongly. r = 0.567, nearly linear climb from mean violence 2.3 (d=0) to 9.0 (d=10). The trend is consistent with no plateau effect, unlike gamification.
📐
How much stronger is this than gamification?
R² = 32.1% vs 10.7% — dehumanization explains 3× more variance. The slope is also twice as steep: +0.735 vs +0.370 per score point.
🖼️
What image type drives both scores highest?
Casualty content (d=4.59, v=7.78) and drone strike (d=5.91, v=6.81). Propaganda memes (d=4.30) also score high — "ГРУZ 200" death markers and blood imagery push dehumanization sharply.
📉
Is there a safe "low" zone?
Yes — at d=0 the mean violence is only 2.28, the lowest of any score level. Civilian content and "other" images cluster here. The floor is much lower than for gamification (which starts at 3.1).
🧩
Is dehumanization a mediator for other scores?
Yes. Its correlations with gamification (0.58), narrative framing (0.59), and aestheticization (0.43) suggest it sits at the centre of the score network — other scores partly work through dehumanization to predict violence.
🧮
What does the equation say?
ŷ = 2.69 + 0.74x — baseline violence at d=0 is 2.69 (lower than gamification's 3.67). The steeper slope means a score-9 image is predicted at 9.33 — near the ceiling.

Cell 1 — Regression & Stats
from scipy import stats import numpy as np df_c = df[['dehumanization_score', 'implied_violence_score']].dropna() x, y = df_c['dehumanization_score'].values, df_c['implied_violence_score'].values slope, intercept, r, p, se = stats.linregress(x, y) print(f"ŷ = {intercept:.4f} + {slope:.4f} × dehumanization_score") print(f"R = {r:.4f} | R² = {r**2:.4f} | p = {p:.2e}")
Cell 2 — Scatter + Regression Line
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt sample = df_c.sample(3000, random_state=42) xj = sample['dehumanization_score'] + np.random.uniform(-0.3,0.3,len(sample)) yj = sample['implied_violence_score'] + np.random.uniform(-0.3,0.3,len(sample)) fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(10,6), facecolor='#0a0e1a') ax.set_facecolor('#0f1628') ax.scatter(xj, yj, alpha=0.12, s=12, color='#005BBB') xl = np.linspace(0,10,100) ax.plot(xl, intercept+slope*xl, color='#FFD500', lw=2.5, label=f'ŷ={intercept:.2f} + {slope:.3f}x R²={r**2:.3f}') ax.tick_params(colors='#7a90b8') for sp in ax.spines.values(): sp.set_edgecolor('#1a2a4a') ax.set_xlabel('Dehumanization Score', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_ylabel('Implied Violence Score', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_title('Dehumanization vs Implied Violence', color='#e8eef8') ax.legend(facecolor='#0f1628', labelcolor='#e8eef8') plt.tight_layout(); plt.show()
Cell 3 — Mean Line + 95% CI Ribbon
means = df_c.groupby('dehumanization_score')['implied_violence_score'].agg(['mean','std','count']) ci = 1.96 * means['std'] / np.sqrt(means['count']) fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(10,5), facecolor='#0a0e1a') ax.set_facecolor('#0f1628') ax.plot(means.index, means['mean'], 'o-', color='#005BBB', lw=2, ms=7) ax.fill_between(means.index, means['mean']-ci, means['mean']+ci, alpha=0.2, color='#FFD500') ax.tick_params(colors='#7a90b8') for sp in ax.spines.values(): sp.set_edgecolor('#1a2a4a') ax.set_xlabel('Dehumanization Score', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_ylabel('Mean Implied Violence', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_title('Mean Implied Violence by Dehumanization Level (95% CI)', color='#e8eef8') ax.set_xticks(range(11)); plt.tight_layout(); plt.show()

ct = df.groupby('content_type')[['dehumanization_score','implied_violence_score']].mean() ct['count'] = df.groupby('content_type').size() fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(10,7), facecolor='#0a0e1a') ax.set_facecolor('#0f1628') ax.scatter(ct['dehumanization_score'], ct['implied_violence_score'], s=ct['count']/ct['count'].max()*2000, color='#005BBB', edgecolors='#FFD500', lw=1.2, alpha=0.8) for idx, row in ct.iterrows(): ax.annotate(idx,(row['dehumanization_score'],row['implied_violence_score']), fontsize=9,ha='center',va='bottom',color='#e8eef8') ax.tick_params(colors='#7a90b8') for sp in ax.spines.values(): sp.set_edgecolor('#1a2a4a') ax.set_xlabel('Mean Dehumanization Score', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_ylabel('Mean Implied Violence Score', color='#7a90b8') ax.set_title('Content Types — Dehumanization vs Violence (bubble = count)', color='#e8eef8') plt.tight_layout(); plt.show()
